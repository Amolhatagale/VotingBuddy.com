Steps to be followed

1

REGISTRATION

First have to register yourself as a voter. This typically involves filling out a registration form with your personal information such as your name, mobile no, email address, date of birth and password.

2

LOGIN

After registering, the next step is to login to your account on the voting website. This typically involves entering your email address, date of birth and a password that you have entered in the registration process. Once you have logged in, you will be directed to the voting page where you can cast your vote.

3

VOTE

Once you have logged in, you will be directed to the voting page where you can cast your vote. Typically, you will be presented with a list of candidates you can vote for anyone candidate in that list.
